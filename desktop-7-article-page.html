The encounter erupted in Jaddan Batta Village in Kastigarh area around 2am when terrorists opened fire on security search parties.
In a recent incident in Doda district of Jammu and Kashmir, two soldiers were injured during a gunfight with terrorists. The encounter took place in a remote area, highlighting the ongoing security challenges in the region. The soldiers were engaged in a counter-terrorism operation when they came under fire, leading to an exchange of gunfire
Both injured soldiers were immediately evacuated to a nearby medical facility for treatment. Security forces have cordoned off the area and are conducting search operations to neutralize any remaining threats
This incident underscores the persistent efforts by security personnel to maintain peace and stability in Jammu and Kashmir amidst the ongoing threat of terrorism .
